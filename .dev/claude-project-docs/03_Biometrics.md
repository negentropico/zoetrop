## Critical Insights

### What Worked
- Elite cardiovascular fitness achieved (54 BPM RHR)
- Recovery capacity excellent (78.5% avg)
- Sleep architecture maintained (7+ hours)
- HRV significantly improved from baseline (32ms M1 → 43ms M3)

### What Failed
- Training volume crashed 73% (Jul→Aug)
- Resistance training insufficient for lean mass preservation
- Volume decline coincided with lean mass loss
- Cessation/resumption transition disrupted training consistency

### 2026 Focus
1. **Sustained Volume**: 5-6 hours weekly minimum
2. **Resistance Priority**: 4x weekly progressive overload
3. **Recovery Leverage**: Use excellent recovery capacity for higher volume
4. **HRV Monitoring**: Adjust intensity based on daily readings
