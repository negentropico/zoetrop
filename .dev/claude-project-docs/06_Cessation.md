## Background & Genetic Context

- **Status**: Lower activity FAAH enzyme
- **Effect**: Slower anandamide (endocannabinoid) breakdown
- **Implication**: Extended timeline required for metabolic clearing

- **FAAH-Adjusted Recommendation**: **120+ days minimum**

## Phase 1: Acute Withdrawal (Days 1-21)

### Expected Symptoms
- Sleep disruption (insomnia, vivid dreams)
- Irritability, anxiety
- Appetite changes
- Cravings

### Sleep Support (ADHD-Adjusted Protocol)

*See 04_Supplements.md Tier 2 Sleep Stack for complete protocol.*

**Key Cessation-Specific Notes:**
- Melatonin 0.3mg (NOT 3-10mg) — chronobiotic, not sedative
- **Extended through Day 120+** (FAAH variant requires prolonged support)
- Avoid GABAergics (valerian, hops, passionflower) — ASD paradox risk
- Add glycine/taurine at Week 4+ (not during acute phase)

### Mood & Anxiety Support
| Supplement | Dosage | Timing |
|------------|--------|--------|
| **NAC** | 600mg × 2 | Morning + evening |
| **Rhodiola Rosea** | 150mg | Morning |
| **Omega-3** | 3g | With meals |

### Behavioral Interventions
- Exercise: Daily minimum 20-30 min walking
- Sleep schedule: Strict 7.5+ hour target, <30 min variation
- Hydration: 1 gallon daily
- Avoid: Caffeine after 12pm, alcohol

### Training (Phase 1)
- Light resistance: 2-3x weekly (avoid overtraining stress)
- Moderate cardio: 3-4x weekly
- Focus: Routine establishment, not intensity

---

## Phase 2: Stabilization (Days 22-60)

### Adjustments
- Melatonin: Reduce to 1-2mg if sleep improving
- L-Theanine: Reduce to as-needed only
- NAC: Continue 600mg 2x daily
- Rhodiola: Continue daily

### Additions
- Structured stress management: 10 min meditation/breathing daily
- HRV monitoring: Adjust training based on recovery scores
- Gradual training volume increase

### Training (Phase 2)
- Resistance: Increase to 3-4x weekly
- Progressive overload: Begin tracking strength gains
- Volume target: 3-4 hours weekly by Day 60

**Note**: Unlike standard protocols, do NOT taper melatonin before Day 120 due to FAAH variant.

### Monitoring Focus
- HRV trends (expect improvement 60-90+ days)
- Sleep quality (should stabilize 60-90 days)
- Recovery scores (normalize 90-120 days)
- Training capacity (should increase steadily)

### Training (Phase 3)
- Full progressive overload protocol
- Volume: 5-6 hours weekly
- Intensity: Appropriate for lean mass recovery
