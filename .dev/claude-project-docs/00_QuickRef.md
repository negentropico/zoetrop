## Critical Reminders

### Timing
- **Melatonin**: 0.3mg @ 8:30 PM (chronobiotic, NOT sedative)
- **Caffeine**: <200mg before 10 AM only (CYP1A2 slow)
- **NAC**: Away from protein meals
- **Bedtime**: 9:00-9:30 PM target

### Safety (See 07_Safety for full list)
- **No GABAergics** - ASD paradox risk (~50%)
- **No hepatotoxins** - NAFLD 98th percentile
- **No B-vitamin mega-doses** - Previous toxicity
- **No methyl donors** - COMT intermediate

### Phase-Specific (Cessation)
- Phase 1 (Days 1-21): Light training, establish routine
- Phase 2 (Days 22-60): Gradual increase, HRV monitoring
- Phase 3 (Days 61-120): Full progressive overload
- Phase 4 (Days 121+): Tier 1 supplements only

## Sleep Troubleshooting

| Issue | Check | Action |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Can't fall asleep | Melatonin timing | Ensure 8:30 PM (3+ hrs before bed) |
| Waking mid-night | Glycine/Mag | Add glycine 3g, mag 300mg at 8 PM |
| Not refreshed | Sleep duration | Target >7.5 hrs |
| HRV crashed | Recovery | Reduce training, prioritize sleep |
