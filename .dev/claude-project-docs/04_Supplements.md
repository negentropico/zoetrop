## Key Protocol Decisions (Why This Stack)

### Why Melatonin 0.3mg @ 8:30 PM
- ADHD delayed circadian phase (~90 min)
- 0.3mg = physiological chronobiotic dose (not sedative 3-10mg)
- Timing 3+ hours before DLMO shifts phase
- Standard advice (30 min before bed) is wrong for ADHD

### Why Avoid GABAergics
- ~50% of ASD individuals show paradoxical excitation
- Altered chloride transporter function
- Glycine/Taurine safer (different mechanism)

### Why 120+ Day Cessation
- FAAH lower activity = slower anandamide clearance
- First attempt (76 days) was insufficient
- Standard 30-60 days doesn't apply to this genetics

### Why No AG1/B-Complex
- May 2025: B6 102.9 μg/L (2x upper limit)
- May 2025: Biotin 7.78 ng/mL (elevated)
- Eliminated AG1, avoid B-vitamin excess until verified

### Why Vitamin E 800 IU Critical
- NAFLD 98th percentile genetic risk
- Combined with chronic NSAID history (pre-M1)
- Primary hepatoprotection alongside NAC, choline
