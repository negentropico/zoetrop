## Progressive Overload Strategy

### Weekly Progression
- **Strength Days**: Add 2.5-5 lbs when hitting top of rep range
- **Hypertrophy Days**: Add reps first, then weight
- **Deload**: Every 4th week at 60% volume

### Rep Range Targets
| Goal | Rep Range | Rest Period |
|------|:---------:|:-----------:|
| **Strength** | 4-6 | 3-4 min |
| **Hypertrophy** | 8-12 | 1.5-2.5 min |
| **Endurance/Pump** | 12-20 | 1-1.5 min |

## Recovery-Based Adjustments

### HRV Monitoring Protocol

| HRV Status | Adjustment |
|------------|------------|
| **Green (>45 ms)** | Full intensity, progressive overload |
| **Yellow (35-45 ms)** | Moderate intensity, maintain volume |
| **Red (<35 ms)** | Light movement only, active recovery |

**Current Status (Jan 1, 2026)**: HRV 33.7 ms = **RED ZONE**
- Cessation Day 10 (started Dec 23, 2025)
- Recommendation: Light movement, active recovery during acute cessation phase
- Expected improvement: HRV should increase as acute phase ends (Day 21 = Jan 12)

### Sleep-Based Adjustments

| Sleep Quality | Adjustment |
|---------------|------------|
| **<6 hours** | Skip heavy compounds, light session |
| **6-7 hours** | Reduce volume 20%, maintain intensity |
| **>7 hours** | Full training as programmed |

## Nutrition Timing for Training

### Pre-Workout (1-2 hours before)
- Complex carbs: 40-60g
- Protein: 25-30g
- Fat: Minimal

### Intra-Workout (Long Sessions >60 min)
- BCAA: 5g (optional)
- Water: 500-750ml

### Post-Workout (Within 1 hour)
- Protein: 40-50g
- Carbs: 50-80g
- **Leucine**: 3g (muscle protein synthesis trigger)

### Daily Protein Distribution
- **Target**: 215g daily (1.3g/lb)
- **Distribution**: 35g × 6 meals
- **Timing**: Every 3-4 hours

---

## Deload Protocol

### Every 4th Week
- Volume: 60% of normal
- Intensity: 70-80% of normal loads
- Focus: Form, mobility, recovery
- Cardio: Maintain Zone 2, skip HIIT
